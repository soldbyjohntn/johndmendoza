About John

Land, commercial & investment real estate — done right

As a licensed real estate broker serving Sevier County, Blount County, and South Knoxville, I specialize in land, commercial, and investment properties with a focus on helping clients pursue strategic development and value-add opportunities.

I work closely with builders, developers, and private investors to navigate every stage of the real estate process — from land acquisition and site due diligence to financing and construction.

I have deep familiarity with local zoning codes, development regulations, and the planning commission process — particularly in Sevier County, where many projects hinge on precise coordination with local authorities. I also assist clients in assessing utility access, including securing sewer connections or septic permits when needed.

In addition to identifying viable sites, I support clients by running financial projections, obtaining contractor quotes, and making lender introductions to ensure each project is properly capitalized and executable. My work is hands-on, detail-oriented, and focused on results.

"Whether you're building from the ground up or repositioning an underutilized asset, I bring the local expertise and transactional insight to help you move forward with confidence."
